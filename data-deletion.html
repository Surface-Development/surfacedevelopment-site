Data Deletion

How to request deletion of your personal data

Surface Development respects your right to control your personal data.

Requesting Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data associated with this app, please email:

privacy@surfacedevelopment.co.uk

Please include:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • The authentication provider you used (Google, Facebook, or Apple)
  • A brief statement requesting data deletion

What Happens Next

We will verify your request and delete your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, subject to any legal obligations requiring retention.

Third-Party Services

Some data may be managed by third-party services (such as authentication or advertising providers). Deletion requests will apply to data controlled by Surface Development.

Contact

If you have questions about data deletion, contact: privacy@surfacedevelopment.co.uk
